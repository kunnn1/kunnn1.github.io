Projects

TokenConduct

TokenConduct is a semantic similarity detector designed to identify and measure the semantic similarity between seperate crypto whitepapers. The primary goal of TokenConduct is to detect potential plagiarism and ensure the semantic originality of technical white papers in the cryptocurrency domain, with the task of addressing the common crypto fraud assoicated with fake ICOs. The detector uses natural language processing, specifically pre-trained BERT embeddings, and yields a similarity threshold between the relevant documents

Quant Calculator

Quant Calculator is my Python-built quantitative calculator project that allows users to input multiple stock tickers, date ranges, and risk-free rates to calculate and visualize integral financial metrics that every investor should utilze including Sharpe Ratio, Sortino Ratio, and Maximum Drawdown through dynamic data fetching. it also provides a graph data visualization of cumulative returns via a .csv file, making it a conducive tool of financial analysis for any investor or trader.
